# Armor System

Defensive equipment for damage reduction.

## Equipment Slots

### Helmet
- Damage reduction
- Additional max health

### Body Armor
- Higher damage reduction
- Essential for later dungeons and bosses

## Upgrade Tiers

Same tier system as tools (4 color tiers, 8 total levels):

| Tier | Color | Levels |
|------|-------|--------|
| 1 | Red | 1-2 |
| 2 | Green | 3-4 |
| 3 | Purple | 5-6 |
| 4 | Black | 7-8 |

## Upgrade Effects

| Level | Damage Reduction | Bonus Health |
|-------|-----------------|--------------|
| 1 | 5% | +10 |
| 2 | 10% | +20 |
| 3 | 15% | +30 |
| 4 | 20% | +45 |
| 5 | 25% | +60 |
| 6 | 30% | +80 |
| 7 | 35% | +100 |
| 8 | 40% | +125 |

## Upgrade Materials
Same as tools - uses world-specific wood and metal resources.

## Upgrade Priority
2nd priority after sword - survivability is important for harder content.
